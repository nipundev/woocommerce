## Accessibility

Your extensions must meet the [Web Content Accessibility Guidelines](https://www.w3.org/WAI/standards-guidelines/wcag/)Â (WCAG). Meeting 100% conformance with WCAG 2.0 is hard work; meet the AA level of conformance at a minimum.
